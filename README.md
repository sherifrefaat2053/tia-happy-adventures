# Tiya's Happy Adventures 🎮✨

A simple, interactive, and colorful web-based game designed specifically for a 3-year-old toddler named **Tiya** (the hero of the game). 

## 🎯 Game Concept & Mechanics (For Codex)
- **Objective:** Colorful balloons and butterflies float up or move across the screen. When Tiya taps/clicks on them, they pop with a fun animation, play a cheerful sound, and award a star (score).
- **Target Audience:** 3 years old. The game must be extremely simple, visually engaging, and impossible to "lose" or get a "game over" to keep it positive and encouraging.
- **Controls:** Tap on screen (for mobile/tablet) or click (for desktop). No complex keyboard controls.

## 🎨 Visuals & Character (Tiya)
- **Hero:** Tiya. She will have a dedicated character sheet. She can stand at the bottom or center, reacting happily (cheering, jumping) whenever a balloon pops.
- **Theme:** A beautiful, bright garden with vibrant colors (pinks, purples, soft blues, and greens).
- **UI:** Big, easy-to-tap elements. Score displayed as glowing stars instead of just boring numbers.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack Constraints
- **Languages:** HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript (All self-contained or neatly structured).
- **Responsiveness:** Must be 100% responsive and work perfectly on both iPad/Tablets and Mobile screens (Touch-friendly).
- **Performance:** Lightweight, no heavy frameworks, fast loading.
